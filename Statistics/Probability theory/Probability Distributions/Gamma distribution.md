title:Gamma distribution

A gamma distribution is a continuous probability distritbution that is used to model postive-valued random variables. It is characterized by two parameters: shape parameter $(\alpha)\,$ and rate parameter $(\beta)$. $\alpha$ determines the shape of the distribution, while the $\beta$ parameter determines the rate at which the distribution decays

$$
X\sim\Gamma(\alpha,\beta)\equiv Gamma(\alpha, \beta)
$$
